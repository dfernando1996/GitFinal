Ingredients
Milk
Eggs
Bread
Scallions
Cheese
Salt
Sweet corn
Pandebono
Cilantro
Pepper

Preparation
1. Heat a pot over medium heat.
2. Add the scallions and let them cook for two minutes.
3. Add the milk.
4. Add salt and pepper to the taste.
5. Reduce the heat and add the eggs.
6. Cook for two minutes or until the eggs are cooked. 
7. Add the bread and pandebono over the changua and garnish with the chopped cilantro. 
8. Serve
